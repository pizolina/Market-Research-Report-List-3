<p><p>Unmanned Marine Vehicles come in various types, including Remotely Operated Surface Vehicles (ROSV) and Autonomous Surface Vehicles (ASV). ROSVs are controlled remotely by operators and are commonly used for tasks such as underwater exploration and research. ASVs, on the other hand, operate autonomously without human intervention, using pre-programmed routes and sensors to navigate the water surface. Both types of unmanned marine vehicles play a significant role in various industries, including defense, research, and commercial applications.</p></p>
