<p><p>Peristaltic pumps for the food and beverage market come in two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps are designed with rollers that squeeze the tubing to create pressure and move the liquid through the system. Shoe type peristaltic pumps, on the other hand, use a shoe or block to compress the tubing and push the liquid forward. Both types are commonly used in the food and beverage industry for their hygienic and gentle pumping capabilities.</p></p>
