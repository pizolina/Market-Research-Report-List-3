<p><p>Medical rubber products can be classified into two main categories: natural rubber products and artificial rubber products. Natural rubber products are derived from the latex of rubber trees and are known for their elasticity and durability. They are commonly used in medical gloves, catheters, and surgical tubing. Artificial rubber products, on the other hand, are synthetic materials that mimic the properties of natural rubber. They are used in a wide range of medical devices such as syringes, seals, and gaskets. Both types of rubber products play a crucial role in the healthcare industry for ensuring patient safety and infection control.</p></p>
