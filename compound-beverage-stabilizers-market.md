<p><p>Compound beverage stabilizers are used to maintain the stability and quality of beverages. The market is segmented into types such as Gum Arabic, Carboxymethyl Cellulose, Xanthan Gum, Carrageenan, and Others. Gum Arabic is a natural stabilizer derived from the Acacia tree. Carboxymethyl Cellulose is a synthetic stabilizer that helps prevent sedimentation. Xanthan Gum is a microbial gum that enhances the viscosity of beverages. Carrageenan, derived from seaweed, is used for thickening and stabilizing beverages. Other stabilizers include pectin, alginate, and guar gum.</p></p>
