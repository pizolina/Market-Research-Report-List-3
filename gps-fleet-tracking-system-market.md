<p><p>The GPS fleet tracking system market can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions involve installing hardware and software directly onto a company's servers, allowing complete control and customization. However, maintenance and upgrades can be time-consuming. On the other hand, cloud-based solutions operate through the internet, allowing access to real-time data from any device with an internet connection.
