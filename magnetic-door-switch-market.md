<p><p>Embedded switch and surface mount switch are two types of magnetic door switches commonly used in the market. Embedded switch is installed inside the door frame, making it invisible from the outside, providing a cleaner and more aesthetically pleasing appearance. On the other hand, surface mount switch is mounted on the surface of the door frame, making it easier to install and replace. Both types of switches serve the same purpose of detecting when a door is opened or closed, but differ in their installation methods and visibility.</p></p>
