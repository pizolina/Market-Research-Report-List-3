<p><p>Electrical Impedance Tomography (EIT) devices are used to monitor changes in electrical conductivity in the body. Static EIT devices provide a snapshot of internal changes, making them suitable for monitoring conditions like lung function or brain activity. Dynamic EIT devices continuously monitor changes over time, making them ideal for applications such as monitoring blood flow or detecting abnormalities during surgery. Both types of EIT devices offer valuable insights into the body's conductivity, allowing for non-invasive monitoring and diagnosis of various medical conditions.</p></p>
