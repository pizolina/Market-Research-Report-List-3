<p><ul><li>Water Atomized Copper Powder</li><li>Gas Atomized Copper Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Atomized copper powder is produced using two main methods: water atomization and gas atomization. Water atomized copper powder is created by spraying molten copper through a water stream, resulting in fine, spherical particles. Gas atomized copper powder is produced by spraying molten copper through a high-velocity gas stream. Both types of atomized copper powder are widely used in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace due to their high purity, uniform particle size, and excellent flow characteristics.</p></p>
