<p><p>The water purifier pre-filter market offers various types of filters to meet different needs. Laminated filters consist of multiple layers of filtration media, providing thorough purification. Straight-through filters offer a direct water flow path and are suitable for high-flow applications. Siphon filters use a siphon principle to separate heavy impurities from water.
