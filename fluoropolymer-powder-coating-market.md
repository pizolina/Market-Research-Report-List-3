<p><p>Fluoropolymer powder coatings come in two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic powder coatings can be melted and reformed multiple times without losing their properties, making them easy to work with. Thermosetting powder coatings, on the other hand, cure when exposed to heat, creating a durable and hard finish. Each type of powder coating offers unique benefits and characteristics, catering to different industrial applications and specific requirements within the market.</p></p>
