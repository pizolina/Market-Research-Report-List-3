<p><p>Telecom cable market types consist of External Copper Telecom Cable, Internal Telecom & Data Cable, and Fiber Optic Cable. External copper cables are used for outdoor telecommunications infrastructure. Internal cables are designed for indoor use, carrying both telecommunications and data signals. Fiber optic cables use glass or plastic fibers to transmit data via pulses of light, offering high bandwidth and faster data transfer speeds. Each type serves specific purposes, with fiber optic cables offering the highest performance and reliability for modern telecom networks.</p></p>
