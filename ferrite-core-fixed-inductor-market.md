<p><p>Ferrite Core Fixed Inductors are classified into two main types - Multi-layer Type and Wire Wound Type. Multi-layer Type inductors are constructed using multiple layers of ferrite material, offering high inductance values in a compact size. Wire Wound Type inductors are made by winding copper wire around a ferrite core, providing high current handling capabilities. Both types are commonly used in electronic circuits for filtering, signal processing, and voltage regulation applications due to their ability to store and release energy in the form of magnetic fields.</p></p>
