<p><p>Heat insulation films are designed to reduce heat transfer through windows, improving energy efficiency in buildings. Endothermic films absorb and dissipate heat, providing an extra layer of protection against temperature fluctuations. Reflective films, on the other hand, reflect heat away from the windows, preventing it from entering the building. Both types of films are effective in reducing energy costs and maintaining comfortable indoor temperatures. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific insulation needs and preferences.</p></p>
