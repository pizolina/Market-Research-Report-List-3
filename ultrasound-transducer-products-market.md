<p><strong>The Ultrasound Transducer Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear</li><li>Convex</li><li>Phased Array</li><li>Endocavitary</li><li>CW Doppler</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound transducer products market offers various types of transducers used in medical imaging. Linear transducers produce high-resolution images and are ideal for superficial imaging. Convex transducers provide a wider field of view and are best for abdominal and obstetric imaging. Phased array transducers offer real-time imaging of the heart and blood vessels. Endocavitary transducers are inserted into body cavities for detailed imaging. Continuous wave (CW) Doppler transducers measure blood flow velocity. Lastly, there are other types of transducers available that cater to specific imaging needs in various medical applications.</p></p>
