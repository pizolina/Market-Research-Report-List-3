<p><p>Silicone Mould Release Agents are categorized into three main types - Polyalkylene Glycol (PAG), Polyethylene Glycol (PEG), and others. PAG-based release agents are known for their high lubricity and low surface tension, making them ideal for applications where a thin film is required. PEG-based release agents are preferred for their water solubility and ease of cleaning. Other types of release agents may include silicone-based or non-silicone alternatives, each offering unique properties for specific applications in the market.</p></p>
