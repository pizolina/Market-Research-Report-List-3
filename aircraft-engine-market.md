<p><p>The aircraft engine market consists of various types including Turboprop Engine, Turbofan Engine, Turboshaft Engine, and Piston Engine. Turboprop engines use a gas turbine to drive a propeller, ideal for small to medium-sized aircraft. Turbofan engines have a large fan at the front which provides most of the thrust, commonly used in commercial airliners. Turboshaft engines are used in helicopters to drive the rotor blades. Piston engines are typically found in smaller general aviation aircraft, using reciprocating motion to generate power.</p></p>
