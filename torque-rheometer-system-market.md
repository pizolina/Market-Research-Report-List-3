<p><p>Torque rheometer systems are used to measure the viscosity and behavior of materials under shear flow. There are two main types of torque rheometers in the market - single screw rheometers and twin screw rheometers. Single screw rheometers are designed for simpler materials testing and are suitable for a wide range of applications. Twin screw rheometers, on the other hand, offer higher precision and are often used for more complex materials such as blends and compounds. Both types of systems play an important role in research and quality control processes across various industries.</p></p>
