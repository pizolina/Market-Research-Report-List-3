<p><p>Inductors for power lines come in various types based on their construction and material used. Winding inductors are made by wrapping wire around a core. Laminated inductors have layers of thin metal sheets stacked together. Film sheet inductors are made using thin sheets of film material. Braided inductors are constructed by weaving together strands of wire. These different market types offer a variety of options for power line inductors, each with its own benefits and applications.</p></p>
