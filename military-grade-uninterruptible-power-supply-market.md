<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Standby</li><li>On-line</li><li>Line-Interactive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military Grade Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems are classified into three main types: Passive Standby, On-line, and Line-Interactive. Passive Standby UPS systems rely on a bypass switch that automatically transfers the load to the battery upon power failure. On-line UPS systems continuously provide power to the load from the battery, offering maximum protection against power disturbances. Line-Interactive UPS systems maintain power through an automatic voltage regulator that adjusts input voltage levels. Each UPS type is designed to provide varying levels of protection and efficiency based on specific military requirements.</p></p>
