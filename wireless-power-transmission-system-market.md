<p><p>Wireless Power Transmission System market includes different types such as Inductive Coupling, Resonant Inductive Coupling, Capacitive Coupling, and Others. Inductive Coupling transfers power using magnetic fields between two coils. Resonant Inductive Coupling enhances efficiency and distance of power transfer through resonance between coils. Capacitive Coupling uses electric fields to transfer power between devices. The Others category includes emerging technologies like radio frequency (RF) systems. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries.</p></p>
