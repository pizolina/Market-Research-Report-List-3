<p><p>A float sensor, also known as a float switch, is a device used to detect the level of liquid within a tank. The sensor works by using a float that rises or falls with the liquid level, triggering a switch to turn on or off accordingly. Float sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing for monitoring and controlling liquid levels.</p><p>The Float Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period. <p><p>The Float Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Top-mounted Type and Side-Mounted Type. Top-mounted Type float sensors are installed at the top of a container or tank and detect the liquid level from above. On the other hand, Side-Mounted Type float sensors are fitted on the side of the container or tank and sense the liquid level through the walls. These different types cater to various applications and requirements, offering flexibility and versatility in liquid level detection.</p></p>
