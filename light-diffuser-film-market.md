<p><p>Light diffuser film is used in various applications such as lighting fixtures, signage, and displays to create a more evenly distributed light output. In the market, there are two main types of light diffuser films: multi-sheet and single-sheet. Multi-sheet diffusers consist of multiple layers of diffusing material, providing higher light transmission and control over light distribution. On the other hand, single-sheet diffusers are simpler in design and provide a more direct diffusion of light. Both types offer different benefits and cater to different lighting requirements.</p></p>
