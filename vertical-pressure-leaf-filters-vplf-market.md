<p><p>Vertical Pressure Leaf Filters (VPLF) with Dry Cake Discharge are designed to remove impurities from liquids by passing them through a series of vertically stacked leaves. The dry cake discharge option is suitable for applications where the filtered solids need to be kept dry for easy disposal. On the other hand, VPLF with Wet Cake Discharge is ideal for situations where the filtered solids can be directly discharged as wet cake. Both types cater to different industry needs and provide efficient filtration solutions.</p></p>
