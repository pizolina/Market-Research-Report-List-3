TWS (True Wireless Stereo) Bluetooth headset chips are designed for use in wireless earbuds that come in pairs and operate independently. On the other hand, Non-TWS Bluetooth headset chips are used in traditional Bluetooth headsets that are connected by a wire or neckband. Both types of chips cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the Bluetooth audio market.</p></p>
