<p><p>Transparent OLED screens are a cutting-edge display technology that allows for see-through visuals. There are two main types of Transparent OLED screens on the market: AMOLED screens and PMOLED screens. AMOLED screens, or Active Matrix OLED screens, offer higher resolution and faster response times, making them ideal for high-end applications. On the other hand, PMOLED screens, or Passive Matrix OLED screens, are more cost-effective and suitable for smaller screens. Both types of transparent OLED screens are revolutionizing industries such as retail, automotive, and entertainment.</p></p>
