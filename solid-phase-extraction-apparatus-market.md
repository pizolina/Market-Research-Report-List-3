<p><p>Solid phase extraction (SPE) apparatus is a sample preparation technique used in analytical chemistry to separate and purify target compounds from complex matrices. It is widely employed in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, food and beverage, and forensic science.</p><p>The SPE apparatus consists of a solid phase cartridge, a sample loading port, and an elution device. The sample is loaded onto the solid phase cartridge, followed by the selective extraction of target compounds. The extracted compounds are then eluted using solvents of varying polarity.</p><p>The global solid phase extraction apparatus market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period.
