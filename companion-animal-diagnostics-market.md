<p><p>Companion Animal Diagnostics market encompasses various types of diagnostic technologies used for pets' health assessment. Clinical biochemistry involves analyzing biochemical parameters in blood samples to detect organ dysfunction. Urinalysis involves examining urine samples to evaluate kidney function and detect urinary tract infections. Immunodiagnostic methods test for infectious diseases through antibody-antigen reactions. Hematology examines blood samples to evaluate blood cell counts and detect blood disorders. Molecular diagnostics involve analyzing genetic materials to identify genetic diseases or assess genetic predispositions. These techniques aid in ensuring companion animals' overall health and timely detection of diseases.</p></p>
