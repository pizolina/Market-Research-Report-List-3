<p><ul><li>Trailed</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stone Separator market is primarily divided into two types: Trailed and Rotary separators. Trailed separators are designed to be towed behind a tractor and are ideal for clearing stones and debris from fields. On the other hand, Rotary separators use rotating blades to separate stones from soil by pushing them to the surface for easy collection. Both types of separators are popular choices among farmers and agricultural workers for their efficiency in removing stones and preserving soil quality.</p></p>
