<p><strong>The COC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ROMP Process</li><li>mCOC Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COC resin market includes two main types of processes - ROMP and mCOC. ROMP process involves ring-opening metathesis polymerization to produce COC resins with highly controlled molecular structures, making them suitable for various applications such as packaging, electronics, and medical devices. On the other hand, mCOC process involves the modification of COC resins through chemical reactions to enhance their properties like thermal stability and flexibility, expanding their use in industries like automotive and construction. Both processes contribute to the diverse applications of COC resins in the market.</p></p>
