<p><p>Polyolefin masterbatches are categorized into three main types: PE masterbatches, PP masterbatches, and others. PE masterbatches are used for coloring and enhancing properties of polyethylene products. PP masterbatches are specifically designed for polypropylene applications, offering improvements in color, processing, and performance. The "others" category includes masterbatches for other polyolefin materials like polybutene or copolymers. Each type of masterbatch is formulated to meet the specific requirements and characteristics of the respective polyolefin material.</p></p>
