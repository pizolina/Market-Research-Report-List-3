<p><ul><li>Cast Camshaft</li><li>Forged Steel Camshaft</li><li>Assembled Camshaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile camshaft market can be categorized into three types: cast camshaft, forged steel camshaft, and assembled camshaft. Cast camshafts are made by pouring molten metal into a mold, resulting in a cost-effective option suitable for most applications. Forged steel camshafts are created by shaping heated steel with a forging press, offering higher strength and durability for high-performance engines. Assembled camshafts are built by combining separate cam lobes onto a common shaft, providing flexibility in design and customization options.</p></p>
