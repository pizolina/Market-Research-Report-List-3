Hydraulic jacks are known for their high lifting capacity and stability, making them popular for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Pneumatic jacks are lighter and more portable, making them ideal for situations where mobility is important. Both types of jacks play a crucial role in maintenance and repair operations for aircraft.</p></p>
