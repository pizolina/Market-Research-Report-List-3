<p><strong>The Protein Assays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-Binding Assays</li><li>Copper-Ion-Based Assays</li><li>Test Strip-Based Assays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Protein assays refer to the techniques used to determine the concentration or presence of proteins in a sample. Different types of protein assays exist. Dye-binding assays utilize specific dyes that bind to proteins, resulting in a color change that can be measured spectrophotometrically. Copper-ion-based assays involve the use of copper ions to react with proteins, forming a complex that can be detected for quantification. Test strip-based assays involve immunoassay techniques, where proteins in the sample bind to specific antibodies on a test strip. Other types of protein assays may utilize alternative methodologies or technologies for protein quantification.</p></p>
