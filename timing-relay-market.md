<p><strong>The Timing Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On-delay</li><li>Off-delay</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Timing relays are devices used to control the timing of an electrical circuit. The On-delay timing relay type is used to delay the activation of a circuit after a signal is received. The Off-delay type is used to delay the deactivation of a circuit after a signal is removed. Other types of timing relays include Interval timing relays, which control the duration of activation, and Recycler timing relays, which repeatedly cycle between on and off states. These different types cater to various industrial and automation needs.</p></p>
