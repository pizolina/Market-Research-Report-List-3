<p><p>Corrugated pallets are classified into three main types based on the number of layers they have: less than 3 layers, 3-5 layers, and others. Pallets with less than 3 layers are simpler in design and are typically used for lighter loads. Those with 3-5 layers offer more strength and durability, making them suitable for moderate weight loads. The "others" category includes specialty pallets with unique features or materials. Each type caters to specific market needs and requirements for safe and efficient material handling.</p></p>
