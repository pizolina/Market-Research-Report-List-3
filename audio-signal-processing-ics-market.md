<p><p>Audio Signal Processing ICs market consists of two main types: Analog Signal Processing IC and Digital Signal Processing IC. Analog Signal Processing ICs manipulate continuous signals in a direct manner through analog circuits. They are known for their accuracy and high-quality sound processing. On the other hand, Digital Signal Processing ICs analyze and modify digital signals using algorithms. They offer flexibility and efficiency in processing audio signals. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the quality of audio output in various electronic devices.</p></p>
