<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antibody Library Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Phage Display</li><li>Ribosome Display</li><li>Yeast Display</li><li>Mammalian Cell Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antibody library technologies are used to generate large collections of antibodies for therapeutic research. Phage display involves inserting antibody genes into bacteriophages, allowing the display of diverse antibody fragments. Ribosome display uses an in vitro translation system to exhibit antibodies on ribosomes. Yeast display employs yeast cells to present antibody libraries on their surface. Mammalian cell display utilizes mammalian cells to express antibodies and display them on their surface. These technologies provide powerful methods for the selection, screening, and isolation of specific antibodies for various applications in healthcare and biotechnology.</p></p>
