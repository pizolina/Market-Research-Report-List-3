<p><strong>The Emulsion PVC Paste Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Micro Suspension Method</li><li>Emulsion Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emulsion PVC Paste Resin is produced using two main methods - Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. In the Micro Suspension Method, small particles of PVC resin are suspended in water with the help of stabilizers and dispersing agents. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves polymerizing vinyl chloride monomer in water with the help of emulsifiers and initiators. Both methods result in the production of PVC paste resins that can be further processed into various PVC products.</p></p>
