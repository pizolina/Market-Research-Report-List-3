<p><p>Infertility services refer to a wide range of medical procedures, treatments, and interventions aimed at assisting couples or individuals who have difficulties in conceiving a child. Several factors contribute to this market growth. Additionally, growing awareness regarding infertility treatments and advancements in medical technology are driving the demand for infertility services.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising use of ART, such as in vitro fertilization (IVF), as the primary treatment option for infertility is also propelling market growth. IVF has proven to be a successful technique for assisted reproduction, and advancements in this technology, such as preimplantation genetic screening (PGS) and intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI), have further improved success rates.</p><p>Moreover, the latest trends in the infertility services market include the increasing incorporation of artificial intelligence and robotics in infertility procedures. These technologies help enhance the accuracy and efficiency of treatments, resulting in improved outcomes.
