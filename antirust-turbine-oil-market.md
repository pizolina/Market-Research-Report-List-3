<p><p>Antirust Turbine Oil can be classified into two main types based on their alkalinity levels - Neutral and Alkalinity. On the other hand, Alkalinity Turbine Oil has a higher pH level, providing better protection against acidic conditions, corrosion, and rust. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and efficient operation of turbines in various industries.</p></p>
