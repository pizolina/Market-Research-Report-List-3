<p><p>Laminated glass interlayer films are used in various industries for safety and security purposes. Two common types of interlayer films are EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate) and PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral). EVA interlayer films provide excellent clarity, UV resistance, and adhesion properties, making them ideal for architectural and decorative applications. On the other hand, PVB interlayer films are known for their high strength and energy absorption capabilities, making them suitable for automotive and construction industries where impact resistance is crucial.</p></p>
