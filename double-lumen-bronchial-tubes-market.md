<p><p>Double lumen bronchial tubes are medical devices used in lung isolation procedures during thoracic surgeries. These tubes have two separate lumens, one for ventilation and another for lung deflation or isolation. The ventilation lumen supplies oxygen-rich gas to the lung while the isolation lumen collapses or seals off the lung, preventing the exchange of air between the two lungs.</p><p>The double lumen bronchial tubes market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
