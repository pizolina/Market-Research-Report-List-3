<p><ul><li>CTS (Conversion Track System)</li><li>TTS (Trailed Track System)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Agricultural Equipment Rubber Track Market includes two main types: CTS (Conversion Track System) and TTS (Trailed Track System). CTS involves converting the existing wheels of the equipment into tracks for enhanced performance on various terrains. On the other hand, TTS consists of tracks that are attached to the equipment as a separate unit, allowing for easy removal and installation. Both CTS and TTS offer increased traction, reduced ground compaction, and improved maneuverability for agricultural machinery.</p></p>
