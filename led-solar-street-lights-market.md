<p><p>LED Solar Street Lights come in two main market types: Standalone and Grid Connected. Standalone systems operate independently of the main power grid, utilizing solar panels to charge batteries for nighttime use. These systems are ideal for remote locations without access to traditional electricity sources. Grid Connected systems, on the other hand, are connected to the main power grid and can supplement or even feed excess energy back into the grid. This type is popular in urban areas where solar energy can be used to reduce electricity costs.</p></p>
