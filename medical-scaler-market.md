<p><p>Medical scalers are dental instruments used to remove plaque and tartar from teeth. There are two main types of medical scalers in the market: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive medical scalers use magnetic fields to create ultrasonic vibrations in a metal stack, while piezoelectric scalers use crystals to generate high-frequency vibrations. Both types are effective in removing debris from teeth and are popular among dental professionals for their efficiency and precision in cleaning teeth.</p></p>
