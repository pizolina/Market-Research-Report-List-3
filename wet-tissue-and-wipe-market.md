<p><p>The wet tissue and wipe market can be segmented based on the technology used in their production. Spunlace technology involves creating nonwoven fabrics through high-pressure water jets. Airlaid technology uses air to create a soft and absorbent material. Wetlaid technology utilizes a water-based process to form the wet wipes. Spunlaid technology involves melting and extruding plastic pellets to form a fine fiber material. Each technology offers unique characteristics and benefits that cater to different consumer preferences in the market.</p></p>
