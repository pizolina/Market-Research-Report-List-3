<p><p>In the pump laser protector market, two main types of lasers are commonly used: singlemode and multimode. Singlemode pump lasers offer a narrow, focused beam of light that is ideal for long-distance transmission and high data rates. Multimode pump lasers, on the other hand, use a larger core size to transmit multiple light signals simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances and lower data rates. Both singlemode and multimode pump lasers provide critical protection for pump lasers in various applications, ensuring reliable and efficient performance.</p></p>
