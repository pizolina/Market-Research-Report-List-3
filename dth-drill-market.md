Flat market is characterized by DTH drill bits with a flat surface, ideal for medium-hard rock formations. Convex market involves DTH drill bits with outward-curved shape, suitable for hard rock formations. Other types of DTH drill markets may include hybrid shapes or specialized designs for specific drilling conditions.</p></p>
