<p><p>Thermoplastic powder coatings are divided into two main market types: general powder coating and functional powder coating. General powder coating is primarily used for decorative purposes, providing a wide range of colors and finishes for various applications such as automotive, consumer goods, and building materials. On the other hand, functional powder coating is specifically designed to enhance the performance and durability of the coated surfaces, offering benefits such as corrosion resistance, UV protection, and improved adhesive properties. Both market types play a crucial role in the overall thermoplastic powder coatings industry.</p></p>
