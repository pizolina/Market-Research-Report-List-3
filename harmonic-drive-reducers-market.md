<p><p>Harmonic Drive Reducers are commonly available in three main types: Cup Style, Hat Style, and Pancake Style. Cup Style reducers have a cup-shaped outer housing that envelops the gear mechanism. Hat Style reducers feature a hat-shaped outer housing that covers the gear mechanism. Pancake Style reducers have a flat, compact design with the gear mechanism arranged in a disc shape. Each style offers unique advantages in terms of size, weight, and efficiency, making them suitable for different applications in robotics, aerospace, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
