<p><p>The Leakage ELISA Kit market offers various types of ELISA assays for different applications. Direct ELISA involves the detection of antibodies or antigens without the need for secondary antibodies. Sandwich ELISA captures the target antigen between two antibodies, enhancing sensitivity. Indirect ELISA utilizes secondary antibodies for signal amplification. Competitive ELISA competes between a labeled antigen and an unlabeled antigen for binding to limited antibodies. Each type of ELISA provides specific advantages and is used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and clinical diagnostics, catering to different research and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
