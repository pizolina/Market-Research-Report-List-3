<p><strong>The Epoxy Glass Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Contact Production Process</li><li>Dry Press Forming Production Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Epoxy Glass Steel market types can be categorized based on the production process used. Wet contact production process involves saturating glass fibers with epoxy resin to create the final product, while dry press forming production process involves stacking layers of glass fabric and epoxy resin in a mold and applying heat and pressure to cure the material. Both methods result in strong and durable epoxy glass steel products used in various applications such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
