PVC Type coding inks are best suited for marking on PVC materials, while XLPE Type is ideal for cross-linked polyethylene surfaces. THHN Type coding inks are designed for thermoplastic high-heat resistant materials, and Nylon Rubber Type inks work well on nylon and rubber substrates. Each type caters to specific market needs for effective product marking.</p></p>
