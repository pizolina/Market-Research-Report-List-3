<p><p>Lithium Cobaltate comes in two main market types: spherical and layered. Spherical Lithium Cobaltate is manufactured in a ball-like shape, making it ideal for use in battery cathodes due to its high energy density and stability. On the other hand, layered Lithium Cobaltate is composed of stacked layers of cobalt and lithium ions, offering high performance in terms of energy density and power output. Both forms of Lithium Cobaltate have their unique advantages and are widely used in various industries for their superior battery performance.</p></p>
