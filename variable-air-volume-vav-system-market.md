<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems offer energy-efficient ventilation solutions for buildings. The Single-Duct VAV system controls the airflow through a single duct, adjusting the volume based on demand. Dual-Duct VAV systems use two separate ducts for hot and cold air, providing more precise control over temperature. Fan-Powered VAV systems include a fan to assist with air distribution. Other types of VAV systems may include series fan-powered, parallel fan-powered, and induction VAV systems, each offering unique features for optimizing air flow in different environments.</p></p>
