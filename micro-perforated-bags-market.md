<p><p>Micro perforated bags are available in various materials such as HDPE, BOPP, PA, CPP, and other materials. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) bags are known for their durability and strength, making them suitable for packaging heavy items. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) bags offer excellent clarity and high tensile strength, ideal for displaying products. PA (Polyamide) bags are known for their high barrier properties, making them suitable for storing perishable goods. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) bags are flexible and offer good moisture resistance. Other materials include PET and PVC bags.</p></p>
