<p><p>The Industrial Automation Software market can be categorized into two types: Cloud Based and On Premise. Cloud Based refers to software solutions that are hosted and accessed over the internet, allowing users to access and control automation processes remotely. On the other hand, On Premise software is installed and run directly on local servers, providing greater control and security but requiring maintenance and updates to be handled by the organization. Both types offer benefits and considerations to businesses based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
