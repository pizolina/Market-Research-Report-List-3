<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete dye is available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based dyes are eco-friendly, easy to use, and have low odor. They are ideal for indoor applications and are popular for their versatility in creating vibrant colors. On the other hand, solvent-based dyes are more durable and water-resistant, making them suitable for outdoor use. They are known for their ability to penetrate deep into the concrete surface, providing long-lasting color. Both types offer a wide range of color options for enhancing the appearance of concrete surfaces.</p></p>
