<p><p>Wood chips can be divided into two main market types: soft wood chips and hard wood chips. Soft wood chips are derived from coniferous trees and are known for their relatively fast decomposition rate, making them ideal for landscaping and gardening applications. Hard wood chips, on the other hand, come from deciduous trees and are prized for their long-lasting properties, making them popular for mulching and ground cover in areas where durability is key. Both types contribute to the thriving wood chips market catering to various consumer needs.</p></p>
