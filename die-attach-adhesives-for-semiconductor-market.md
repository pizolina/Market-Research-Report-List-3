<p><p>Die Attach Adhesives for Semiconductor Market includes three main types: Insulating Type, Sintering Type, and Heat-curing Type. Insulating Type adhesives provide electrical insulation between the die and substrate. Sintering Type adhesives use a high-temperature process to bond the die to the substrate, creating a strong and durable connection. Heat-curing Type adhesives require heat to cure and form a solid bond between the die and substrate. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
