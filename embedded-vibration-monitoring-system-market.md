<p><p>The embedded vibration monitoring system market consists of various types such as accelerometers, proximity probes, velocity sensors, transmitters, and others. Accelerometers are used to measure acceleration while proximity probes monitor the distance between the probe and target. Velocity sensors measure the speed of an object in motion. Transmitters are used to transmit vibration data. Other types may include sensors and monitoring devices that serve unique purposes in tracking and analyzing vibrations in different applications.</p></p>
