<p><p>Leg compression machines are devices designed to improve blood circulation in the legs. There are three main types of leg compression machines: Continuous Compression, Intermittent Compression, and Sequential Compression. Continuous Compression machines apply consistent pressure to the legs, while Intermittent Compression machines alternate between high and low pressure. Sequential Compression machines consist of multiple chambers that inflate in a specific sequence to mimic the natural movement of blood through the veins. Each type of machine offers unique benefits for addressing circulation issues in the legs.</p></p>
