<p><p>Structural adhesives are categorized into four main types: epoxies, toughened acrylics, polyurethanes, and cyanoacrylates. Epoxies offer high strength and are commonly used in industrial applications. Toughened acrylics provide flexibility and impact resistance. Polyurethanes offer a balance of strength and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. Cyanoacrylates, commonly known as super glues, provide fast curing and strong bonds. Each type of structural adhesive agent has its own unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
