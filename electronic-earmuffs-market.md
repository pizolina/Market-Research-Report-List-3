<p><strong>The Electronic Earmuffs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Earmuffs</li><li>Active Earmuffs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive earmuffs are designed to block out loud noises by using thick padding to absorb sound. They do not have any electronic components and rely solely on their construction to provide protection. Active earmuffs, on the other hand, use electronic technology to amplify low-level sounds while still providing protection from high-level noises. They often include features such as volume control and sound enhancement, making them popular among hunters and shooters. Both types of earmuffs serve the same purpose of protecting the wearer's ears from loud noises, but active earmuffs offer additional features for enhanced listening capabilities.</p></p>
