<p><p>Hot Strip Mill and Cold Rolling Mill are two important processes in the production of steel and other metal products. This process allows for the production of large quantities of steel with uniform thickness and surface finish.</p><p>On the other hand, Cold Rolling Mill involves the reduction of the thickness of steel sheets through a series of rolling stands at room temperature.
