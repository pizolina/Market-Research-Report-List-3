<p><strong>The Graphene Based Supercapacitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrical Double Layer Capacitors</li><li>Pseudocapacitor</li><li>Asymmetric Supercapacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphene-based supercapacitors are categorized into three main types based on their energy storage mechanisms. Electrical Double Layer Capacitors store energy through the separation of charges at the electrolyte-electrode interface. Pseudocapacitors utilize fast and reversible redox reactions at the electrode surface to store energy. Asymmetric supercapacitors combine both mechanisms to achieve high energy and power densities. These different types cater to various applications by offering a balance between energy storage capacity and charge/discharge rates.</p></p>
