<p><p>Near Field Sensor Market Types include Inductive Sensors, Capacitive Sensors, Magnetic Sensors, Photoelectric & Ultrasonic Sensors, and Optical Sensors. Inductive Sensors detect metal objects without physical contact. Capacitive Sensors measure changes in electrical capacitance. Magnetic Sensors detect changes in magnetic fields. Photoelectric & Ultrasonic Sensors use light or sound waves to detect objects. Optical Sensors use light beams to detect objects. Each type of sensor has its own unique application and uses in various industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
