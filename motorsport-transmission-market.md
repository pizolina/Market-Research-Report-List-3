<p><ul><li>H-Gearbox</li><li>Sequential Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorsport transmission market types can be categorized into two main types: H-Gearbox and Sequential Gearbox. H-Gearbox refers to a traditional type of transmission that features an "H" pattern shift lever, allowing drivers to manually shift gears by moving the lever in a horizontal pattern. On the other hand, Sequential Gearbox is a more advanced type of transmission that enables sequential shifting, meaning the gears are selected in a sequential order, typically using paddles or electronic levers. This allows for quicker and more precise shifting, making it popular in high-performance motorsports.</p></p>
