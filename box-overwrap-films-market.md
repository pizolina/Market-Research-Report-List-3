<p><p>Box overwrap films are used to enclose and protect boxes during storage and transportation. Shrink films are made from polymer plastic that shrinks when heat is applied, tightly wrapping around the box. This type of film is commonly used for consumer goods packaging. On the other hand, stretch films are made from stretchable plastic that can be stretched and wrapped around the box without the need for heat. It provides secure containment and is commonly used in industrial and pallet packaging. Both shrink and stretch films serve the purpose of ensuring the stability and protection of boxed products.</p></p>
