<p><p>The financial service application software market consists of two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It allows financial service providers to access and manage their data and applications from any location, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-efficiency. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed and operated on the local infrastructure of the organization.
