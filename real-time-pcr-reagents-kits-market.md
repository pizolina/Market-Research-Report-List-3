<p><p>Real-Time PCR reagents and kits are essential tools used in molecular biology research for quantifying DNA or RNA samples. There are two main types of Real-Time PCR markets: One-step Real Time PCR and Two-step Real Time PCR. One-step Real Time PCR kits combine reverse transcription and PCR amplification in a single tube, providing a convenient and streamlined process. In contrast, Two-step Real Time PCR kits separate reverse transcription and PCR amplification steps, offering greater flexibility and control over experimental conditions. Both types of kits are valuable for accurate and efficient gene expression analysis.</p></p>
