<p><strong>The Wafer Processing ESCs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wafer Processing ESCs Market consists of two main types - Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type ESCs use electrostatic force generated by charges accumulated on the wafer surface, while JR Type ESCs utilize a combination of Coulomb and van der Waals forces. Both types are crucial in wafer processing equipment, ensuring precise positioning and handling of wafers during various processes. The choice between the two types depends on specific application requirements such as wafer size, material, and processing conditions.</p></p>
