<p><p>Thermal imaging cameras can be categorized into three main types based on the wavelengths they detect: short-wave, mid-wave, and long-wave. Short-wave cameras are best for high-temperature applications, while mid-wave cameras are ideal for medium to high-temperature targets. Long-wave cameras are typically used for lower temperature applications and offer excellent resolution. Each type of camera has its own strengths and weaknesses, making them suitable for different types of thermal imaging needs.</p></p>
