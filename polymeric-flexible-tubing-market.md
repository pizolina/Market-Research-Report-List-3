<p><p>Polymeric Flexible Tubing Market Types include Thermosetting Elastomers, which are materials that undergo a chemical reaction to form a network structure and are known for their high heat resistance and durability. Thermoplastic Resins (Non-Elastomeric) are materials that soften when heated and harden when cooled, offering good chemical resistance. Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) are a class of materials that combine the characteristics of thermoplastics and elastomers, providing flexibility and resilience. Each type of tubing offers unique properties catering to different application needs.</p></p>
