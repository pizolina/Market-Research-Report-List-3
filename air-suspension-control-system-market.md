<p><ul><li>Capsule</li><li>Membrane</li><li>Compound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air Suspension Control System market can be categorized into three types: Capsule, Membrane, and Compound. Capsule systems use a small container filled with air to adjust suspension height. Membrane systems utilize a flexible membrane to control air pressure and therefore suspension height. Compound systems combine the features of both capsule and membrane systems for better control and performance. Each type offers unique advantages and cater to different needs in the market, providing options for manufacturers and consumers alike.</p></p>
