<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Exchange</li><li>Indirect Heat Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Iron ore pellets dryers are classified into two main types: direct heat exchange and indirect heat exchange. </p><p>In direct heat exchange dryers, hot gases directly contact the pellets to transfer heat, while indirect heat exchange dryers use a separate medium, such as hot air or steam, to transfer heat to the pellets. </p><p>Direct heat exchange dryers are more efficient and have lower energy costs, while indirect heat exchange dryers are preferred for sensitive materials that may be damaged by direct contact with hot gases.</p></p>
