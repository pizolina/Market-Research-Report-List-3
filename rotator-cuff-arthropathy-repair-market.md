<p><p>Reverse Total Shoulder Replacement is a type of surgery used for patients with severe rotator cuff arthropathy where the traditional total shoulder replacement may not be effective. Muscle/Tendon Transfer involves moving a healthy muscle or tendon to replace the damaged rotator cuff. Partial Joint Replacement replaces only the damaged part of the joint. Other techniques may include arthroscopic debridement or injections. Each type of repair has its own indications and benefits depending on the extent of the rotator cuff arthropathy.</p></p>
