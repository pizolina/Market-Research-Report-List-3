<p><strong>The Indoor Instrument Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current Transformer</li><li>Potential Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current Transformer and Potential Transformer are two key types of Indoor Instrument Transformers used in electrical systems. Current Transformers are used to measure current in high voltage circuits by transforming higher currents to a lower, more manageable level for measuring instruments. Potential Transformers, on the other hand, are used to step down high voltage levels to lower levels for measurement purposes. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate measurement and protection of electrical equipment in indoor settings.</p></p>
