<p><strong>The RF Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF filters are crucial components in wireless communication devices that help in eliminating unwanted frequencies while allowing the desired signals to pass through. Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters are widely used for their high performance in terms of bandwidth and insertion loss. On the other hand, Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters are gaining popularity due to their ability to handle high-power applications and enable miniaturization of devices. Both SAW and BAW filters cater to different market segments based on their unique advantages and applications.</p></p>
