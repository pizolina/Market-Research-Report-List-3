<p><strong>The Ion-Exchange Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Membrane</li><li>Homogeneous Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion-exchange membrane market is divided into two types - Heterogeneous Membrane and Homogeneous Membrane. Heterogeneous Membrane consists of a porous support layer with an ion-exchange coating, allowing for higher selectivity and stability. On the other hand, Homogeneous Membrane is a single-layer membrane with uniform ion-exchange properties throughout, providing consistent performance. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the application requirements, such as separations in the chemical industry, water treatment, and energy storage.</p></p>
