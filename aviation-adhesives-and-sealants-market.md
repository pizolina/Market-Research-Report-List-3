<p><p>Aviation adhesives and sealants market can be categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based adhesives use solvents as a carrier to bond materials together, while water-based adhesives use water as a carrier. Solvent-based adhesives are known for their strong bonding capabilities and resistance to harsh environments, making them ideal for aircraft applications. On the other hand, water-based adhesives are preferred for their environmental friendliness and easy cleanup, making them a popular choice for manufacturers looking to reduce their carbon footprint.</p></p>
