<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric</li><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Pyroelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic ceramics are categorized into three main types based on their properties and applications: ferroelectric, piezoelectric, and pyroelectric. Ferroelectric ceramics exhibit spontaneous electric polarization that can be reversed by an external electric field, making them ideal for applications such as capacitors and sensors. Piezoelectric ceramics generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, commonly used in sensors, actuators, and transducers. Pyroelectric ceramics respond to changes in temperature by producing an electric polarisation, used in infrared detectors and thermal imaging devices.</p></p>
