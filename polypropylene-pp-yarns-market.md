<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) yarns are widely used in various industries due to their durable and lightweight properties. Polypropylene Multifilament Yarn is composed of multiple filaments twisted together, making it strong and suitable for applications like ropes, bags, and geotextiles. On the other hand, Polypropylene Monofilament Yarn consists of a single filament, making it smoother and more flexible for use in products like fishing nets, conveyor belts, and filter fabrics. Both types of PP yarns offer unique characteristics and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
