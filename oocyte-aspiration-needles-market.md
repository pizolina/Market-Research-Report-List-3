<p><strong>The Oocyte Aspiration Needles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Lumen</li><li>Double-lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oocyte aspiration needles are commonly available in two main types: single lumen and double lumen. Single lumen needles feature a single tube for the retrieval of oocytes, whereas double lumen needles have two tubes - one for flushing and the other for aspiration. Single lumen needles are simpler in design and more cost-effective, while double lumen needles are preferred for their ability to provide simultaneous flushing and aspiration, allowing for a more efficient oocyte retrieval process. Both types of needles play a crucial role in assisted reproductive technology procedures.</p></p>
