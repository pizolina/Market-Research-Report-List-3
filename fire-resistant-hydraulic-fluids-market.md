<p><strong>The Fire Resistant Hydraulic Fluids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fire resistant hydraulic fluids are categorized into four main types - HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-based with added anti-corrosion agents, HFB fluids are water-based with higher oil content for better lubrication, HFC fluids are water glycol-based with improved fire resistance, and HFD fluids are synthetic ester-based with superior fire resistance and biodegradability. Each type offers varying levels of fire protection and performance, catering to specific hydraulic system requirements in industries where fire safety is crucial.</p></p>
