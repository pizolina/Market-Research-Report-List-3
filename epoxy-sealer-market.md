<p><p>Epoxy sealer market is divided into two main types: water based and solvent based. Water based epoxy sealers are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, have low VOC emissions, and are often preferred for indoor applications. On the other hand, solvent based epoxy sealers provide a tougher finish, better adhesion to the surface, and are commonly used for outdoor applications or in high traffic areas. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
