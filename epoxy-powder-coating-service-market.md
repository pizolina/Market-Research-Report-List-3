<p><p>Epoxy powder coating is a form of protective finish applied to various surfaces. The market is divided into two types: fast curing and common curing. Fast curing type involves a quick drying process, allowing for faster production and turnaround times. This type is commonly used in industries where efficiency is crucial. On the other hand, common curing type requires a longer drying time, providing better adhesion and a more durable coating. It is often preferred for surfaces subjected to harsh conditions or where longevity is critical.</p></p>
