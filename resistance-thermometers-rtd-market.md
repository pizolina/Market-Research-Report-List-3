<p><p>Resistance Thermometers (RTD) are sensors used to measure temperature. There are two main types of RTDs: Thin Film Resistance Thermometers and Wire Wound Resistance Thermometers. </p><p>Thin Film Resistance Thermometers have a thin layer of metal film deposited on a ceramic substrate, providing faster response times and better accuracy. </p><p>Wire Wound Resistance Thermometers use a coil of wire wound around a ceramic or glass core, offering more stability and durability in extreme temperature conditions. Both types are widely used in various industries for temperature sensing applications.</p></p>
