<p><ul><li>Microwave</li><li>Millimeter Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>24GHz radar sensors are categorized into two main types: microwave and millimeter wave. Microwave radar sensors operate in the frequency range of 2-40 GHz, providing accurate detection and tracking capabilities. On the other hand, millimeter wave radar sensors operate in the higher frequency range of 30-300 GHz, enabling high-resolution imaging and superior performance in adverse weather conditions. Both types of radar sensors are widely used in automotive, industrial, and military applications for object detection, collision avoidance, and surveillance.</p></p>
