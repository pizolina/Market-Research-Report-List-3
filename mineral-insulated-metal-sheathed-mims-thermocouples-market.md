<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Mineral Insulated Metal Sheathed (MIMS) Thermocouples Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grounded Type</li><li>Ungrounded Type</li><li>Exposed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mineral Insulated Metal Sheathed (MIMS) Thermocouples are available in three main types: Grounded Type, Ungrounded Type, and Exposed Type. Grounded Type thermocouples have the thermocouple welded to the sheath, providing fast response times and high accuracy. Ungrounded Type thermocouples have the thermocouple insulated from the sheath, reducing the risk of electrical interference. Exposed Type thermocouples have the thermocouple directly exposed, allowing for rapid temperature measurement. Each type has its own unique advantages, allowing for a wide range of applications in various industries.</p></p>
