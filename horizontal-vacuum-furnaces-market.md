<p><p>Horizontal vacuum furnaces are classified into three main types based on the number of chambers they have:</p><p>1. Single Chamber Vacuum Furnaces: These furnaces have one chamber for processing materials under vacuum conditions.</p><p>2. Double Chamber Vacuum Furnaces: These furnaces have two chambers, allowing for separate processing and cooling chambers.</p><p>3. Multi Chamber Vacuum Furnaces: These furnaces have multiple chambers for different processing steps, enabling more complex thermal treatments. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and enables efficient and flexible processing of materials.</p></p>
