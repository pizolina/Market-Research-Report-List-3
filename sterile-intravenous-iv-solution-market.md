Crystalloids are salt-based solutions that help replenish fluids and electrolytes in the body. Colloids are made up of larger molecules that stay in the bloodstream longer, helping to increase blood volume. The "others" category includes specialized IV solutions such as albumin and dextran. Each type of IV solution serves a specific medical purpose and is used based on the patient's condition and needs.</p></p>
