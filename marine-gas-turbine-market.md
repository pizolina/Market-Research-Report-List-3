<p><p>Marine Gas Turbines are classified into three main types based on their size and capacity: Aeroderivative Gas Turbines, Light Duty Gas Turbines, and Heavy Duty Gas Turbines. Aeroderivative turbines are derived from aircraft engines and are used for propulsion in smaller ships. Light duty turbines are used for auxiliary power generation and propulsion in mid-sized vessels. Heavy duty turbines are designed for larger ships and applications requiring higher power output. Each type of turbine offers specific benefits and is selected based on the vessel's requirements.</p></p>
