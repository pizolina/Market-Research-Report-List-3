<p><strong>The Road Marking Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Performance-Based Markings</li><li>Paint-Based Markings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road marking materials market can be categorized into performance-based markings and paint-based markings. Performance-based markings use materials like thermoplastic, cold plastic, epoxy, and preformed tapes which offer durability and high reflectivity, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. Paint-based markings, on the other hand, use materials like water-based and solvent-based paints which are cost-effective but less durable. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in road marking applications, with performance-based markings offering better longevity and visibility compared to paint-based markings.</p></p>
