<p><p>Aluminum laminated film is a type of packaging material that combines the benefits of both aluminum and plastic films. The aluminum layer provides strength, rigidity, and high barrier properties, while the plastic film allows for flexibility and heat sealability.</p><p>The Aluminum Laminated Film Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.3% during the forecast period.
