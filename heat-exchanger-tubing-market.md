On the other hand, welded tubes are created by welding multiple pieces together, providing cost-effective solutions for less demanding applications. Both types of tubes are essential components in heat exchangers, allowing for efficient heat transfer and facilitating the exchange of fluids in various industrial processes.</p></p>
