<p><p>Foam rollers come in two main types - hollow foam rollers and solid foam rollers. Hollow foam rollers have a core that is filled with air and is more lightweight and easier to transport. On the other hand, solid foam rollers are made of solid material throughout, providing a firmer surface for deeper muscle penetration. Both types are used for self-myofascial release and muscle recovery, but individuals may prefer one over the other based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
