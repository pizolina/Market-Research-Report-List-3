<p><p>The high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU) system market includes two types: ultrasound-guided and MR-guided. Ultrasound-guided HIFU systems use real-time ultrasound imaging to guide the high-intensity ultrasound beams into the targeted area, allowing precise treatment delivery. On the other hand, MR-guided HIFU systems use magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) to visualize the targeted tissue and provide real-time monitoring during the procedure. Both types offer non-invasive treatment options for various conditions, providing patients with accurate and safe treatment outcomes.</p></p>
