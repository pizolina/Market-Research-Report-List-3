EML devices use electromagnetic signals to locate utilities buried underground, while GPR systems transmit radar pulses to identify subsurface objects. Each technology has its own strengths and limitations, allowing utility mapping professionals to choose the most suitable method based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
