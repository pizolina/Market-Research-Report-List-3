<p><strong>The Solar Radiation Smart Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Cell Type</li><li>Thermopile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar radiation smart sensors are classified into two main types: Silicon cell type and Thermopile type. Silicon cell type sensors function by converting solar energy into electrical current through a semiconductor material, making them highly efficient and cost-effective. On the other hand, Thermopile type sensors work by generating a voltage proportional to the differential temperature between the black absorbing surface and the heat sink, offering accurate measurements of solar radiation. Both types play a crucial role in capturing, monitoring, and analyzing solar radiation data for various applications.</p></p>
