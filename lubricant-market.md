<p><p>Semi-synthetic lubricants are a blend of mineral and synthetic oils, offering improved performance compared to mineral lubricants at a lower cost than full synthetic options. Synthetic lubricants are entirely man-made and provide superior lubrication, cleanliness, and protection for high-performance engines. Mineral lubricants are derived from crude oil and are the most cost-effective option, ideal for standard engine applications. Each type caters to different needs and budgets in the lubricant market, offering varying levels of performance and protection for machinery and vehicles.</p></p>
