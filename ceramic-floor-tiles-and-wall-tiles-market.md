<p><p>Ceramic floor tiles and wall tiles market can be classified into two main types: glazed ceramic tiles and unglazed ceramic tiles. Glazed ceramic tiles are coated with a layer of liquid glass that is then baked onto the surface, giving the tiles a shiny, protective finish. Unglazed ceramic tiles do not have this coating, making them more porous and natural-looking but also more susceptible to staining and wear. Both types of tiles offer a wide range of design options and are popular choices for both residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
