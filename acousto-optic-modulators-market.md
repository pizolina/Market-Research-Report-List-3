<p><p>Acousto-Optic Modulators (AOM) are devices that control the intensity, frequency, and phase of a laser beam by employing sound waves. There are two main types: Fiber-Coupled AOMs and Free-Space AOMs. Fiber-Coupled AOMs are designed to be compatible with optical fiber systems, allowing for easy integration and stable performance. On the other hand, Free-Space AOMs are used in open space environments, providing flexibility in laser beam manipulation. Both types play a crucial role in various applications, including laser communication, laser printing, spectroscopy, and laser material processing.</p></p>
