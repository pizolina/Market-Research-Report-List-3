<p><p>Forklift Pedestrian Warning Systems are available in two main types in the market - RFID Based and UWB Based. RFID Based systems use radio frequency identification technology to detect pedestrians and prevent accidents. UWB (Ultra Wideband) Based systems use short-range radio waves to accurately track the location of pedestrians in real-time. Both systems aim to improve safety in warehouses and industrial settings by alerting forklift operators of nearby pedestrians, reducing the risk of collisions. Each type offers unique features and benefits to suit different operational needs.</p></p>
