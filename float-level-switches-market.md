<p><p>Float level switches are commonly used in industries to monitor and control liquid levels. There are three main types of float level switches: top-mounted, side-mounted, and bottom-mounted. Top-mounted level switches are installed at the top of the tank and are ideal for high-level alarms. Side-mounted switches are attached to the side of the tank and are used for intermediate level control. Bottom-mounted switches are placed at the bottom of the tank and are suitable for low-level alarms. Each type has its own unique application and benefits in the market.</p></p>
