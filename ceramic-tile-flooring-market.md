<p><strong>The Ceramic Tile Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glazed</li><li>Unglazed</li><li>Lapped</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic tile flooring is classified into three main market types: glazed, unglazed, and lapped. Glazed ceramic tiles have a layer of liquid glass applied before firing, resulting in a shiny, moisture-resistant surface. Unglazed ceramic tiles are porous and offer a more natural, textured look but require sealant for protection. Lapped ceramic tiles have overlapping edges that create a unique appearance and are often used for outdoor applications. Each type offers different benefits and aesthetics to suit various design preferences and usage needs.</p></p>
