<p><p>Thermal interface tapes and films are materials used to improve the thermal conduction between components such as electronic devices and heat sinks. Thermal interface tapes are adhesive-based materials that are easy to apply, while thermal interface films are non-adhesive materials that require pressure to form a bond. Both tapes and films are available in various types such as acrylic-based, silicone-based, and polyimide-based, each suited for different applications based on factors like thermal conductivity, adhesion strength, and operating temperature range.</p></p>
