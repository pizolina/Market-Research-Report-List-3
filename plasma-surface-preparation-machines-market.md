<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Pressure Plasma Surface Preparation Machines</li><li>Low Pressure/Vacuum Plasma Surface Preparation Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma surface preparation machines can be categorized into two main types: atmospheric pressure plasma machines and low pressure/vacuum plasma machines. Atmospheric pressure machines operate at normal atmospheric pressure conditions, while low pressure/vacuum machines work under reduced pressure or vacuum conditions. Atmospheric pressure machines are more commonly used for surface cleaning and activation in industries such as automotive and electronics, while low pressure/vacuum machines are preferred for applications requiring more precise control and customization, such as in the semiconductor and aerospace industries.</p></p>
