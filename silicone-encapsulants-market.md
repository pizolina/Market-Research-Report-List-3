<p><p>Silicone encapsulants can be divided into three main types: general encapsulants, optically clear encapsulants, and thermally conductive encapsulants. General encapsulants provide protection and insulation for electronic components. Optically clear encapsulants are specifically designed to maintain optical clarity while providing protection. Thermally conductive encapsulants are formulated to effectively dissipate heat, making them ideal for applications where thermal management is crucial. Each type caters to different needs within the electronics industry, offering varying levels of protection, clarity, and thermal conductivity.</p></p>
