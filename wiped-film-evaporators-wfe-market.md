<p><p>Wiped Film Evaporators (WFE) consist of two main types: Vertical Wiped Film Evaporators and Horizontal Wiped Film Evaporators. Vertical WFE are commonly used for heat-sensitive materials due to their efficient heat transfer capabilities and compact design. Horizontal WFE, on the other hand, are preferred for processing high viscosity liquids and slurries. Both types offer benefits such as high evaporation rates, low operating temperatures, and minimal fouling, making them popular choices in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
