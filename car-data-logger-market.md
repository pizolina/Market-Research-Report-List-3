<p><p>Car data loggers are devices that record real-time data from a vehicle's sensors and systems for analysis and troubleshooting. Three common types of connections for car data loggers are USB, Bluetooth/Wi-Fi, and SD card. USB connection allows for direct data transfer to a computer for analysis. Bluetooth/Wi-Fi connection enables wireless data transfer to mobile devices or computers. SD card connection stores data directly onto a memory card for later retrieval and analysis. Each type of connection offers unique advantages for different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
