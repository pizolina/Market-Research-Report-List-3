<p><p>Fiber optic isolators are devices used to protect fiber optic systems from damage caused by back reflections. There are two main types of fiber optic isolators: sensitive isolators and insensitive isolators. Sensitive isolators are designed to be highly responsive to back reflections, providing maximum protection for delicate components. On the other hand, insensitive isolators are less sensitive to back reflections, making them suitable for applications where high power levels are involved and where protection against back reflections is still necessary. Both types of isolators play a crucial role in maintaining the performance and reliability of fiber optic systems.</p></p>
