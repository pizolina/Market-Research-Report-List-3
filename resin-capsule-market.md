<p><p>A resin capsule is a type of anchoring system widely used in construction and mining industries to secure anchor bolts in place. Resin capsules consist of a two-part resin compound enclosed in a plastic capsule that are inserted into a pre-drilled hole and then activated by a twisting motion to mix the two components, resulting in a strong bonding agent that holds the anchor bolt firmly in place.</p><p>The resin capsule market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for reliable anchoring solutions in construction and mining activities.
