<p><p>Moisture curing polyurethane adhesives are available in two main types: Liquid and Hotmelt. Liquid polyurethane adhesives are applied in liquid form and cure when exposed to moisture in the air. They are commonly used for bonding a variety of materials including plastics, wood, and metals. Hotmelt polyurethane adhesives are applied in molten form and solidify upon cooling. They are often used in applications where a fast curing time is required, such as in the automotive and electronics industries.</p></p>
