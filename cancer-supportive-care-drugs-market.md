<p><p>The Cancer Supportive Care Drugs market encompasses various types of drugs. G-CSFs, or Granulocyte-colony Stimulating Factors, stimulate the production of white blood cells to strengthen the immune system. ESAs, or Erythropoiesis Stimulating Agents, increase red blood cell production to combat anemia. Anti-emetics help manage chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting. Bisphosphonates prevent bone disorders caused by cancer. Opioids provide pain relief, while NSAIDs, or non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, alleviate inflammation and pain. These drugs collectively improve the quality of life for cancer patients by addressing various symptoms and side effects of the disease and its treatments.</p></p>
